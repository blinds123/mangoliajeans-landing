# Phase 1: Buyer Research

Complete this phase before generating any images or writing any copy.

---

## Input Required

- Competitor URL to analyze

---

## Step 1: Visit Competitor URL

Extract:
- Product name and price
- Key selling points
- Target audience language
- Pain points from reviews
- Customer objections

---

## Step 2: Complete the Research Framework

### 2.1 Dream Customer Profile

```
DEMOGRAPHICS:
- Age range: ___
- Gender: ___
- Location: ___
- Income level: ___

PSYCHOGRAPHICS:
- What do they value most? ___
- What are they afraid of? ___
- What embarrasses them? ___
- Who do they want to impress? ___
```

### 2.2 Three Levels of Desire (Russell Brunson)

| Level | Question | Answer |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Surface** | What do they think they want? | ___ |
| **Real** | What do they actually want? | ___ |
| **Deep** | What do they secretly desire? | ___ |

*Example (Sequin Skirt):*
- Surface: "I want a nice skirt for parties"
- Real: "I want to look stunning and get compliments"
- Deep: "I want to feel like the most confident woman in the room"
