## ANTIGRAVITY INJECTIONS (MANDATORY)

### 1. PHASE 6: MARKET EMOTION + ULTIMATE CRAVING
- **Dominant Emotion:** Distill the market's current state (Shame, Fear, Anger, Overwhelm, Envy, Burnout, Rage).
- **Ultimate Craving:** Identify what they are secretly starving for (To feel competent, to be respected, to reclaim time, to escape drudgery).

### 2. PHASE 140: PSYCHOLOGICAL FRAMEWORK CROSS-MAPPING
You MUST apply these models to the strategy:
- **Maslow:** Where does the desire sit? (Safety, Esteem, Belonging).
- **Jobs-to-Be-Done:** What functional/social job is the product being "hired" for?
- **Pain-Dream-Fix:** Define the Bridge.

### 3. PHASE 8: AVATAR SYNTHESIS
- What would make Our Reader say: "This brand gets me"?
- What language makes them feel "Seen"?

### 4. OFFER ARCHITECTURE (COLD AUDIENCE CONVERSION)
Cold audiences convert on **irresistible offers**, not just good copy. Define:
- **The Stack:** Main product + Bonuses (at least 2) + Guarantee + Scarcity element
- **Price Anchoring:** Show higher value BEFORE revealing price (e.g., "Retailers charge $250+")
- **Risk Reversal:** The guarantee must feel like they can't lose (e.g., "30-Day No-Questions")
- **Urgency Trigger:** Why buy NOW? (e.g., "Limited to 250 pieces", "Sale ends midnight")
- **Impulse Purchase Justification:** Give them a "reason to buy today" they can tell themselves

## OUTPUT REQUIREMENT: `strategy_brief.json`
Must include `dominant_emotion`, `ultimate_craving`, `psychological_bridge`, and `offer_stack` fields.
