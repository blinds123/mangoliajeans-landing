## When to use this skill

- Use this to analyze product materials and construction.

- Use this independently of other research.

## DATA SOURCE

Get product specifications from:
1. **Browser scraping** of the product page/URL provided by user
2. **User-provided specifications** if available
3. **Supplier/manufacturer data sheets** if accessible

## ANTIGRAVITY INJECTIONS (MANDATORY)

### 1. OBJECTIVE REALITY CHECK
- **Materials:** What is it *actually* made of? (e.g. 12oz canvas, 100% matte nylon).
- **Physics of Benefit:** How does the physical construction create the claimed benefit? (e.g. "Onion-quilt traps air for 15% better thermal retention without bulk").
- **Flaw Detection:** Where could the product physically fail? (e.g. "Pankou knots require 3x reinforcement to prevent unraveling").

### 2. THE "WITHOUT" FORMULA
- Identify what the product does **without** standard industry compromises.
- (e.g. "Reduces heat-dumping *without* using non-breathable plastic liners").
